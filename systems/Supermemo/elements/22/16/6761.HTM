facelift
中文释义
1.面部拉皮手术(名词，非正式)
2.房屋翻修〔名词，非正式)

英文释义
1. (noun, INFORMAL) Surgical procedure, or series of pracedures, intended to improve one's appearance, usually by tightening lose facial skin that results from aging.
2. (noun, INFORMAL) An improvement in the physical appearance of an old building intended to make it appear more modern.

EXAMPLE: My sister, who will be sixty years odd next month, had a facelift recently and now looks ten years younger.
我的下个月就将年满60岁的姐姐不久前做了一次面部拉皮手术，现在她看上去像是年轻了10岁。
