S-M-<up/down>     (org-timestamp-up/down)
On CLOCK log lines, increase/decrease the timestamp at point and the one of the previous (or the next clock) timestamp by the same duration. For example, if you hitS-M-<up> to increase a clocked-out timestamp by five minutes, then the clocked-in timestamp of the next clock will be increased by five minutes. 
Org-mode, Clocking commands
