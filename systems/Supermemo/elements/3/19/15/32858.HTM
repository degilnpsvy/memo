enum GIOError

GIOError is only used by the deprecated functions g_io_channel_read(), g_io_channel_write(), and g_io_channel_seek().

Members

G_IO_ERROR_NONE

no error

  

G_IO_ERROR_AGAIN

an EAGAIN error occurred

  

G_IO_ERROR_INVAL

an EINVAL error occurred

  

G_IO_ERROR_UNKNOWN

another error occurred
