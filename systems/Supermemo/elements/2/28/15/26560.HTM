A7.7.227 VFMA, VFMS

Floating-point Fused Multiply Accumulate multiplies two single-precision registers, and accumulates the result into the destination single-precision register. The result of the multiply is not rounded before the accumulation.

Floating-point Fused Multiply Subtract negates one single-precision register and multiplies it with another single-precision register, adds the product to the destination single-precision register, and places the result in the destination single-precision register. The result of the multiply is not rounded before the addition.
