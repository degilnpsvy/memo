4.1. View Switching

Key Action

m

Switch to main view.

d

Switch to diff view.

l

Switch to log view.

p

Switch to pager view.

t

Switch to (directory) tree view.

f

Switch to (file) blob view.

B

Switch to blame view.

H

Switch to branch view.

y

Switch to stash view.

h

Switch to help view

S

Switch to status view

c

Switch to stage view
