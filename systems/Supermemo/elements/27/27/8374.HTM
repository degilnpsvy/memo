Calculations

C-c +     (org-table-sum)
Sum the numbers in the current column, or in the rectangle defined by the active region. The result is shown in the echo area and can be inserted with C-y. 
S-<RET>     (org-table-copy-down)
When current field is empty, copy from first non-empty field above. When not empty, copy current field down to next row and move cursor along with it. Depending on the option org-table-copy-increment, integer field values will be incremented during copy. Integers that are too large will not be incremented. Also, a0 prefix argument temporarily disables the increment. This key is also used by shift-selection and related modes (see Conflicts).
