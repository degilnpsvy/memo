Constants

All constants are integers. As in C, the linker considers an integer beginning with 0 to be octal, and an integer beginning with 0x or 0X to be hexadecimal. The linker considers other integers to be decimal.

In addition, you can use the suffixes, K and M, to scale a constant by 1024 or 1024*1024, respectively. For example, the following all refer to the same quantity:

_fourk_1 = 4K;

_fourk_2 = 4096;

_fourk_3 = 0x1000;
