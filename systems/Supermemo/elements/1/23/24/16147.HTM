The pthread_cond_init() function is used to dynamically initialize a condition variable. The circumstances in which we need to use pthread_cond_init() are analogous to those where pthread_mutex_init() is needed to dynamically initialize a mutex (Section 30.1.5); that is,

we must use pthread_cond_init() to initialize automatically and dynamically allocated condition variables, and to initialize a statically allocated condition variable with attributes other than the defaults.
