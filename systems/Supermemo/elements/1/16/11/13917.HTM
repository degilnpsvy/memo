The syntax of a complex conditional of make is as follows: 
     conditional-directive
     text-if-true
     else
     text-if-false
     endif

or: 

     conditional-directive-one
     text-if-one-is-true
     else conditional-directive-two
     text-if-two-is-true
     else
     text-if-one-and-two-are-false
     endif
