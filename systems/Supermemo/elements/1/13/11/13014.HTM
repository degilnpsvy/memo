1.2. What is Success?

First and foremost, don't accept anyone else's definition of success. Spend plenty of time thinking about what the word success means to you. Respect the views of friends, family, teachers, and colleagues, but don't let them dictate your personal goals.

As an exercise, rate each of the following measures of success on a scale of 1 to 10 (1 = not an important measure of success, 10 = most important measure of success), and think about why it's important.

  • Wealth
  • Power
  • Fame
  • Grades
  • Skill
  • Admiration of others
  • Trust of others

What other measures can you think of?
