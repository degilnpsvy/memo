1.1. Motivation

Do you believe that you have a clear understanding of what the workplace is like after college? If not, you should explore this soon. Visit people working in the field to find out what their jobs are like, preferably before deciding on a major. Many students spend years in college, and find out soon after that they don't like working in their field.

Have you given much thought to what you're trying to accomplish during your college career? If you think it's about accumulating knowledge, guess again. You'll eventually realize that the knowledge you gained in college is minimal, and mostly not directly applicable to your work. What you can develop while in college, which will be invaluable after, is learning skills and independence.
