2. Homework Policy

The homework problems at the end of each chapter of the online notes are not collected or graded. You are on your honor to do them on your own, without looking at the solutions.

The homework problems are for your benefit alone, and are the best way to test your knowledge of the material prior to quizzes and exams.

Quizzes and exams are given at the beginning of lab or discussion, and are written with the expectation that you have done the homework problems pertaining to the previous week's lecture. This policy is intended to encourage good homework and study habits. If you have done the homework well, you will likely find the quizzes and exams fairly easy.

Homework problems follow the order of the sections, so simply do them in the order they appear until you reach a question on material that has not been covered yet.
