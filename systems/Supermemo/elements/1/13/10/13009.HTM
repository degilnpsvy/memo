1. Using the Online Notes

These notes are provided as a rough outline for the course, and in some cases to supplement the textbook. They are not a substitute for attending class or reading the book. Students are expected to know all material covered in class, which will include material and details not found in these notes.

In addition to these notes, students should utilize other resources such as online search engines, and Wikipedia. Wikipedia is an especially good source for gaining a quick, basic understanding of many subjects.
