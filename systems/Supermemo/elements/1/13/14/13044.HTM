4.15. Hex and Octal with Signed Numbers

Converting between binary and hex/octal ignores the format of the data. Always convert the digits as if unsigned.

    FF unsigned = 255
    FF 2's comp = -1
    FF 1's comp = -0
    FF bias-127 = +128
    
    100100102 (two's complement) = ?
