On many UNIX implementations, we can use casts such as the following to eliminate warnings from the C compiler:

1funcp = (int (*) (int)) dlsym(handle, symbol);

However, the specification of dlsym() in SUSv3 Technical Corrigendum Number 1 notes that the C99 standard nevertheless requires compilers to generate a warning for such a conversion, and proposes the[...] syntax shown above.
