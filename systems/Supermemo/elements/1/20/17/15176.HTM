When we say “[...]”, we mean that a (the pattern word) must appear either followed by whitespace or at the end of the value in order to be replaced; other occurrences of a in the value are unaltered. For example: 
     foo := a.o b.o c.o
     bar := $(foo:.o=.c)

sets ‘bar’ to ‘a.c b.c c.c’. See Setting Variables.
