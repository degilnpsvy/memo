8.1 # Primitive Channels

SystemC’s primitive channels are known as primitive because they contain no hierarchy, no simulation processes, and are designed simple to be very fast. All primitive channels inherit from the base class sc_prim_channel. Because they are SystemC channels, they must also inherit from and implement one or more SystemC interface classes. The SystemC library contains several built-in primitive channels. This chapter focuses on the simplest channels, sc_mutex, sc_semaphore, and sc_fifo<T>. Additional primitive channel topics will be discussed in later chapters.
