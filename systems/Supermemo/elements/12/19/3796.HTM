Entry point

EJTAG debug, when mapped to “probe” memory. See section 12.1 for some notes on the EJTAG on-chip debug system.

BASE+0x200+...

 Multiple interrupt entry points—seven more in VI mode, 62 in EIC mode.

All others.

 

BASE+0x100

Cache error—in RAM but always through uncached kseg1 window.

Simple TLB Refill (SR(EXL) is zero).
